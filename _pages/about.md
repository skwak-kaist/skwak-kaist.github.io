I am a Senior Researcher at the Media Research Division of the
<span style="color:#1f3a68;">ETRI</span>, based in Daejeon, Korea.
I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from
<span style="color:#1f3a68;">KAIST</span> in February 2026, advised by
Prof. Munchurl Kim ([VICLab](https://www.viclab.kaist.ac.kr/)).

My research lies at the intersection of
<span style="color:#1f3a68;">immersive video</span> and
<span style="color:#1f3a68;">computer vision</span>, with a focus on
3D/4D scene representation, novel view synthesis, and video coding for
machines (VCM). I am particularly interested in dynamic 3D Gaussian
Splatting and learning-based methods for next-generation immersive media
services.

You can reach me at s.kwak [at] etri.re.kr.
