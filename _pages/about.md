I am a Senior Researcher at the **Media Research Division** of the
[Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI)](https://www.etri.re.kr),
based in Daejeon, Korea. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from
**KAIST** in February 2026, advised by Prof. Munchurl Kim.

My research lies at the intersection of **immersive video** and **computer vision**,
with a focus on **3D/4D scene representation**, **novel view synthesis**, and
**video coding for machines (VCM)**. I am particularly interested in dynamic
3D Gaussian Splatting and learning-based methods for next-generation immersive
media services.

You can reach me at **s.kwak [at] etri.re.kr**.
